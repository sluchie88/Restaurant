About our Nook

Tom's Nook was founded in July of 2019. While we are still new, we are fast becoming a neighborhood staple for our small menu but expertly crafted food, our warm and cozy atmosphere, and our frequent events. We host all kinds of events, from bar trivia every Tuesday and Thursday nights (hosted by Matt's Trivia), to our monthly book club, and everything in between. Our goal is to make our neighborhood a better place, and make some good food while we're at it. So come on down and enjoy a frosty pint, bring some friends, and stick around and chat with our friendly staff and patrons. We gurantee you won't be disappointed.
